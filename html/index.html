Creating Synchronicities

The Sacred Cycle of Intention, Attention, and Manifestation

1

Intentions are seeded

Set your Intention, and share it with your community.

2

Tune Your Attention

Focus collective Attention on the most important issues facing your tribe.

3

Action Manifests

Over time, this collective attention accumulates until someone decides to take action to Manifest this intention.

4

Results Are Shared

Documentation of your effort is shared with your tribe - field notes, photos, video updates, etc. in a Shared Resource Vault dedicated to that intention.

5

Blessings Are Made

If others resonate with your work, they bless it with periods of past attention → released into active form as a Token of Gratitude → and placed into your stewardship.

6

The Cycle Repeats

Now place that Token of Gratitude on whatever Intention you now want Manifested, incentivizing action.
